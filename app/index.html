Keyboard Controls: (Select Object)

  • 'w' - Translate-Z Negative (Away).
  • 's' - Translate-Z Positive (Toward).
  • 'a' - Translate-X Negative
  • 'd' - Translate-X Positifve.
  • 'q' - Rotate-Y Positive
  • 'e' - Rotate-Y Negative
  • 'r' - Rotate-X Positive
  • 'f' - Rotate-X Negative
  • 'z' - Rotate-X Positive
  • 'c' - Rotate-X Negative
  • Space - Translate-Y Positifve.
  • Shift - Translate-Y Negative.

Animation Controls: (Rubik's Cube)

  • 'p' - Start / Pause.
  • Note: When cube is selected to move, entire cube spinning annimation will stop.

Mouse Controls: (Camera)

  • Left Click - Rotate.
  • Scroll Wheel - Zoom.
  • Right Click - Pan.
